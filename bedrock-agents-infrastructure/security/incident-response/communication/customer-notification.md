### Immediate Actions (Within 24 Hours)

1. **Change Your Password**

   - Log in to your account
   - Update your password to a strong, unique password
   - Use a password manager if possible
   - Enable two-factor authentication

2. **Review Your Account**

   - Check for unauthorized access
   - Review recent activity logs
   - Verify your account settings
   - Contact support if anything seems suspicious

3. **Stay Alert**
   - Monitor your email for phishing attempts
   - Watch for unauthorized charges
   - Be cautious of unsolicited communications
   - Report suspicious activity immediately

### Recommended Actions (Within One Week)

4. **Update Your Email Security**

   - Change password on associated email account
   - Enable two-factor authentication on email
   - Review connected apps and devices
   - Check recovery phone numbers

5. **Monitor Your Credit**

   - Register for credit monitoring (provided by us)
   - Check your credit reports for fraud
   - Set up fraud alerts with credit bureaus
   - Monitor financial statements
